My Apps

Space RTS

Simple spaced based strategy game for Android. Original idea was for as simple of an RTS as I could make.

Hacker News App & Widget

Hacker news delivered with full themes and widgets. Multiple Hacker News Widgets can be placed on your Android home screen so you can stay up to date. Widgets support theming as well.

Storm Tracker & Widget

Compare weather and storms for different models like HAFS-A, GFS and CMC.

Number Puzzle Widget & Game

Playable game inside of an Android Widget. Sort the nubmers in the puzzle from 1-x. Comes in different sizes like 3x3 and 4x4. Game is playable whenver you want from your home screen.

Tic-Tac-Toe Widget & App

A small playable game, just tic-tac-toe, but in a widget. Can be placed on your homescreen and played whenever you please.
